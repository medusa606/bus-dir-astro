The places on this list have all been vetted by our team on multiple visits, not just once on a good day.
  - heading: The pub roast vs. the restaurant roast
    body: Bristol does both well. The pub roast — ideally in a room with a fire, a good pint on cask, and a slightly chaotic atmosphere — is a different experience from the more composed restaurant version, but not necessarily worse. Some of the best roasts in the city come out of pub kitchens. The key is that the kitchen is genuinely running the food, not just heating it up.
