sections:
  - heading: Smash vs. thick patty — the Bristol divide
    body: Bristol has settled firmly into the smash burger camp over the last few years, and for good reason — the increased surface area means more Maillard reaction, more crust, more flavour. The best smash burgers here use fresh-ground beef with a generous fat ratio and hit a screaming hot flat-top. The result is something fundamentally different from the thick, pink-centred patty style — and in the right hands, much better.
  - heading: The sides matter too
    body: A great burger is nothing without its supporting cast. Several of Bristol's best burger spots put as much thought into their fries, shakes, and sauces as they do the main event. It's worth asking what they're proud of — you'll often find a house sauce, a seasonal side, or a soft serve that's as much of a reason to visit as the burger itself.
